Exchange - Proof of Concept

This is an implementation of a digital exchange, where people can send money to others using their digital signatures.

Upon page load, at the bottom, you will see two sample accounts loaded. The x and y coordinates of the public key for each account are printed out. Each fake wallet has 100 ETH in it.

Below that, the private keys for each account are printed out for each account

You can choose a sample account address, and enter the adddress into "Your Address" for your wallet.

Then, you can enter another amount to send a recipient. Enter the "SEND AMOUNT" and a "RECIPIENT" address (you choose another fake account from those fake account addresses printed out below).

After you click the "TRANSFER AMOUNT" button, the R and S components of the digital signature for the amount you want to send will appear below in "Your Wallet's Digital Signature."
